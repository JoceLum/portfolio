Jocelyn Lum

UX Designer
Hello there!

Throughout the past few years of my experience in the tech industry, one thing has always remained constant: my passion for creating digital experiences that are not only pleasing to the eye, but also intuitive and enjoyable for the user as well. With just over 3 years of web development experience under my belt, I made the transition over to UX design when the opportunity was presented to me early in the spring of 2019.

Being the sole UX designer for a software company that has never had one before presented some unique learning experiences. I played a pivotal role in providing the designs for several of our consumer-facing projects, and became the subject-matter expert for an accessibility initiative for AODA compliance. The former required learning how to use UX design tools on the job, as well as doing extensive research into UX best practices. Outside of work, I keep up with the industry as much as I can by attending UX conferences and talks (FITC, Fluxible, etc). I am also a member of the Interaction Design Foundation, and have taken several courses including “Design Thinking: The Beginner’s Guide” and “Human-Computer Interaction - HCI”.

When I am not preoccupied with latest UX puzzle, I spend most of my free time listening to podcasts and music, cooking, decorating my home, playing with my dog, and going out for trail runs.
